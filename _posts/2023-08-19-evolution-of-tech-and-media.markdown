Ralph and Larry take a breather from the demos and just... talk. With summer winding down and a kid shipped off to Texas, the conversation wanders through old-time radio shows, the legacy of Paul Reubens, and why the cycles of technology keep rhyming with history. From shared mainframes in college to cloud gaming streaming off an Nvidia 4080, they trace the full circle of computing — and wonder whether AI is really all that different from every other disruption that came before it. A relaxed, philosophical episode for anyone who's ever thought "haven't we been here before?"

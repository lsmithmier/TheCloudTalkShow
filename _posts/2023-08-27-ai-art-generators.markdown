Ralph is out, so Larry and Raquel take the wheel for a hands-on tour of AI art generators — and nothing goes smoothly, which honestly makes it more fun. They stumble through Mid-Journey's Discord-only setup, poke around Open Art's sketch-to-image tools, and find Bing Image Creator to be the most accessible of the bunch. Along the way they discover that AI really struggles with Klingons, Larry almost updates his LinkedIn photo to an anime portrait, and Raquel raises some thoughtful questions about where these tools are getting their training data. The episode wraps with a book recommendation that ties it all together: bias in AI isn't just a tech problem, it's a data problem.
